SplashFlow allows users to monitor rivers and streams throughout the globe.
Add Demographic gages to your favorites for quick monitoring of current gauge height, precipitation, and flood stage.

At SplashFlow, we understand the devastating impact floods can have on communities.
That's why we've developed a comprehensive helpline application dedicated to providing timely assistance during flood emergencies.
Our mission is simple: to empower individuals and communities with the resources they need to stay safe and secure during flood events.
